## ğŸ“ Evaluation Criteria

### Practical Exam (70%)
*   **Tekiah (15)**: Tone quality, stability, clear start/stop.
*   **Shevarim (15)**: Evenness of the 3 broken notes.
*   **Teruah (15)**: Clarity of the 9 staccato beats.
*   **Tekiah Gedolah (15)**: Duration (min. 9 seconds) and power.
*   **Ensemble (10)**: Synchronization and dynamics.

*   **Biblical Knowledge (10)**: Understanding key verses.
